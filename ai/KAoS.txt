KAoS is a real-time strategy video game developed by Bungie and published by Microsoft Game Studios. The game was first released on April 9, 2003, for the Xbox console. KAoS is set in a fantasy world where players can choose to play as either the humans or the alien Xel'Jonians in a battle for dominance.

The gameplay of KAoS involves gathering resources, building and managing armies, and engaging in large-scale battles against AI opponents and human players. The game features a variety of unique units, structures, and technologies that allow for diverse strategic playstyles.
